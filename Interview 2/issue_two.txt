Issue: Balance Not Updating After Deposit

The system fails to update the user's account balance correctly after a successful deposit transaction. This results in users seeing incorrect balance information, leading to confusion and potential financial discrepancies.

How to Reproduce the Bug:
1. Login with a valid user account (e.g., Username: john, Password: john, PIN: 1234)
2. Initiate a deposit transaction for a specific amount (e.g., $100)
3. Check the account balance immediately after the transaction
4. Observe that the balance does not reflect the deposited amount

The deposit endpoint must properly update the user's balance in the database and return the correct updated balance in the response.
