Issue: Transfer

The transfer endpoint does not validate the transfer, and is broken.

How to Reproduce the Bug:
1. Login with a valid user account (e.g., Username: john, Password: john, PIN: 1234)
2. Attempt to transfer a amount (e.g., 100) to another account
3. Observe that the balances are not updated and the function is broken.

The transfer endpoint must validate the following:
- The amount is a positive number before processing the transaction.
- The description has been filled out.
- The transfer occurs and the balance is updated.
